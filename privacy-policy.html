Leanbiome Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Leanbiome, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to ensure your privacy.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you when you visit our website, make a purchase, or interact with our services. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Personal identification information such as name, email address, and shipping address.
  • Payment information such as credit card details and billing address.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Process transactions and deliver your orders.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Improve the functionality and user experience of our website.
  • Send promotional materials, including newsletters, if you have opted in.

How We Protect Your Information

We use secure encryption methods to protect your sensitive data and ensure its safety. All transactions are processed through secure servers, and we adhere to industry-standard practices to safeguard your personal information.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with trusted service providers to help us operate our website or process transactions, such as payment processors or shipping companies.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and personalize content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you wish to make changes to the information we have on file or would like to unsubscribe from our communications, please contact us using the details below.
